About the Firm

Serving clients in Lebanon since 1992

Abed Al Karim Hijazi Law Office is a full-service law firm based in Beirut, Lebanon. Since 1992, the firm has provided comprehensive legal services to individuals, businesses, and organisations across Lebanon and the Middle East.


We provide practical legal advice, draft and review contracts, assist clients in resolving disputes, and represent them before Lebanese courts and public authorities.
